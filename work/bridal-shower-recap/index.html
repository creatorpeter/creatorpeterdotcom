About the project

A fun and casual bridal shower celebration photographed in Miami. The brief was to keep things natural and candid — capturing genuine moments and the energy of the event rather than posed shots.

Shot in available light with a documentary approach, the goal was to give the client a gallery that feels real and alive, not staged.
